Projects

The projects below demonstrates my engineering design background. They were all done to solve different problems different countries are facing. They were all done in groups which I was a part of.

Toco Device

This is a high fidelity prototype illustrating a low cost version of a tocodynamometer device.This is used when a woman is in labour and about to give birth.The device is mainly used in monitoring, displaying and warning a medical personnel when complications like uterine rapture are likely to occur.This is so to avoid uterine rapture which leads to deaths of women and babies during child birth in most African countries.

Drones for health project

Drones for health project was to do with building up a UAV drone to assist in transportation of health facilities in remote areas.The image shows a drone built with locally available materials with a few materials imported from outside the country Malawi.

anteater feeding device

A nutritional feeding device for an ant eater at the houston Zoo in USA in its medium fidelity prototype form. Designed to help the ant eater in the zoo to feed well. It is made up of wood laser cut and glued into some maze puzzle format to excite the ant eater when its feeding so as to help prolong its life.
